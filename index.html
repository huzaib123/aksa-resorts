The Man Behind Aksa

Ashaq Hussain
Shangloo

Founder & Host, Aksa Resorts

Born and raised in Srinagar, Ashaq Hussain Shangloo has spent a lifetime building things that carry the warmth and character of Kashmir. From his family's textile legacy in Maharaj Gunj to Ladycare in the heart of Lal Chowk, every venture he has touched reflects a belief in quality, tradition, and honest work.

In 1995, he brought that same belief to Pahalgam — and Aksa Resorts was born. What started as a personal vision has grown into a place where thousands of guests have found rest, beauty, and a genuine taste of Kashmiri living. He still considers every guest at Aksa a personal visitor in his home.

"Pahalgam gave me peace. Aksa is how I share it."

— Ashaq Hussain Shangloo

Year Round

Kashmir in Every Season

Winter

Snow blankets the valley in silence. Pahalgam transforms into a white world — frozen rivers, frost-covered pines, and the kind of quiet that only mountains know. Aksa keeps you warm through all of it.

Spring

The valley wakes up slowly. Wildflowers push through the thaw, the Lidder river runs clear and fast, and the air smells like the earth breathing again. Spring in Pahalgam is something you carry with you long after you leave.

Summer

Long golden days, cool mountain air, and a valley in full bloom. Trekking trails, shepherd meadows, and evenings cool enough for a warm cup of kahwa on the balcony.

Autumn

Chinar trees turn amber, saffron fields glow, and the mountains take on a warmth that feels like a quiet farewell before winter. Autumn is Pahalgam's most gently beautiful season.
